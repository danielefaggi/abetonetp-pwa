Trail 9 - A tour around Abetone passing through the Imposti
A not particularly demanding ring from a technical and physical point of view that allows you to take a nice and fresh walk or a trail training to run around Abetone.

POINTS OF INTEREST
Mount Maiori. A beautiful circular route that winds through fir and beech woods in the Abetone Forest Reserve. Without excessive slopes, it is an ideal route to savor the snow-coated atmosphere of the winter forest
