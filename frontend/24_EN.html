Sentiero 24 - La Malandrinata
From Pracchia to Abetone...
a path that crosses the entire ridge, a path full of emotion but extremely hard with an impressive amount of ups and downs passing through the most beautiful ridges of the Apennines.
Along the climb you come across all types of vegetation typical of the Apennines and you cross places with particular and significant toponyms: the Pedata del Diavolo, the Poggio del Ronco are just some of the points.
...to then reach the Poggio dei Malandrini where the whole world changes shape Finally, the ridge is a succession of paths, lawns and challenging crossings where you need to pay the utmost attention. There are times when it is really difficult to choose where to direct your gaze.
