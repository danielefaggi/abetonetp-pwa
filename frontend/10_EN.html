Trail 10 - Anello del Doni
Glimpses of Lago Scaffaiolo
Simple route around Abetone with passage along the pleasant paths of Viale Regina Elena and Viale Regina Margherita. In summer, the passage through raspberry groves and blackberry brambles makes everything more interesting...
