Trail 11 - The Pian degli Ontani Biogenetic Reserve
Among secular beech trees in search of tranquillity
The route takes place entirely on the forest road that crosses the Biogenetic Reserve and therefore does not present any difficulties from a technical point of view. You travel in the constant company of tall and imposing beech trees that create timeless atmospheres. With a little extra effort, you can reach the famous Prato Bellincioni (1420m).
