Trail 23 - Trail del Malandrino
From Prato to Abetone on the roads of the Malandrini
A dive into history retracing those streets that the Marauders used to cross, smuggling salt in times long gone... starting from the city to reach pristine peaks illuminated by the sun through a unique inner journey in the Tuscan landscape that will always be able to arouse emotions. The watchword is always the same: DO NOT UNDERESTIMATE THE DARK SIDE OF THE APENNINES...

PPOINTS OF INTEREST
Trail del Malandrino. The race was born way back in 1222, the existence of a road is documented which from Orsigna reaches Monteacuto delle Alpi (BO) a communication route between Pistoia and Bologna "highway of the Middle Ages ‚Äù, where people, animals and goods transited. A constantly monitored route, to cross it you had to pay a toll. in fact, the customs were often deceived by smugglers, who faced the paths at night amidst a thousand dangers. Thus was born this race, to revive the adventures of these smugglers. Through a path that winds through the paths of our beautiful Apennines. Today, as then, the departure will be at night and then arrive at Abetone by the afternoon.
