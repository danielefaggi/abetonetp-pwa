Trail 12 - Fra Pian degli Ontani e Pian di Novello
A route that crosses the two villages through beautiful woods
A variegated route which, starting from Pian degli Ontani, climbs up to the forest road inside the splendid beech forest of the Biogenetic Reserve. Continuing inside this imposing wood until you reach Pian di Novello and via a quick descent along the Sestaione stream you reach Pian degli Ontani again.
