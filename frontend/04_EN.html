Trail 4 - Il Libro Aperto
Climbing through the pages of the Open Book
Technically not demanding excursion but which requires good training in consideration of the altitude difference faced in the second part of the route. It is important to remember that climate changes can be extremely sudden in this area, so it is recommended to always be well equipped.

POINTS OF INTEREST
Abetone (1385 meters) in ancient times the whole area was called "boscolungo" due to the presence of a dense forest of fir and beech trees, such as the MAGGIOCIONDOLO, which in spring its blooms stain the mountain slopes yellow, It is a tree with a height of around 7 meters. Today the forest covers approximately 2,700 hectares.
Libro Aperto mountain group formed by two peaks Monte Rotondo (1937 meters) and Monte Belvedere (1896 meters) The peaks of Monte Libro Aperto mark the border between Tuscany and Emilia Romagna.
